Privacy Policy

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This privacy policy informs you about what data our app collects, how we use it, and what rights you have.

What data is collected?

Our app measures water consumption by tapping on the screen. No personal data such as name, address, or location is automatically collected.

We do not store any user data or personal information.

How is the data used?

The data entered in the app is processed exclusively locally on your device to calculate water consumption. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Data sharing with third parties

No data is shared with third parties.

Your rights

You have the right to request information about any data we store and can request deletion at any time. Since we do not store personal data, this usually does not apply.
